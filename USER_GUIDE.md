## 1. Getting Started

### What is Fainzy?

Fainzy is a food delivery app that connects you with local restaurants. You can browse menus, order your favorite meals, and have them delivered to your home, office, or anywhere you are.

### How Do I Get the App?

- Download "Fainzy" from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android)
- The app icon is orange with a white delivery symbol
- Tap "Install" and wait for the download to complete

### Opening the App for the First Time

When you open Fainzy for the first time, you'll see:

1. **Welcome Screen** - A quick introduction to the app
2. **Phone Number Entry** - Where you enter your phone number to sign up or log in

## 2. Setting Up Your Account

### New Users: Creating Your Account

If you've never used Fainzy before, here's how to set up your account:

#### Step 1: Enter Your Phone Number

1. On the "Enter Phone Number" screen, type your phone number
2. Make sure to include your country code (for example: +234 for Nigeria)
3. Tap "Continue"
4. We'll send you a code (OTP) via text message

**Example:** If your number is 08012345678 in Nigeria, enter: `+2348012345678`

#### Step 2: Enter the Verification Code

1. Check your text messages for a 4-digit code
2. The app might automatically read the code for you
3. If not, type the code manually
4. Tap "Verify"

**Didn't receive the code?**
- Wait 30 seconds and tap "Resend Code"
- Make sure your phone has good signal
- Check that you entered the correct phone number

#### Step 3: Fill In Your Details

Since you're new, we need some information to create your account:

| Field | What to Enter | Example |
|-------|---------------|---------|
| First Name | Your given name | John |
| Last Name | Your family name | Doe |
| Email Address | Your active email | john.doe@email.com |
| Password | Create a secure password | MySecurePass123! |

**Password Tips:**
- Use at least 6 characters
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Add at least one number
- Don't use easily guessed passwords like "12345678"

#### Step 4: Tap "Create Account"

You're all set! Now let's find your location so we can show you nearby restaurants.

---

### Returning Users: Logging In

If you've used Fainzy before, it's even easier:

1. Enter your phone number (the one you used before)
2. Enter the verification code we send you
3. You're in! We'll remember your details from last time

**Changed your phone number?**
- Contact Fainzy support to update your account
- You'll need to verify your identity

## 3. Finding Your Location

This is one of the most important steps! We need to know where to deliver your food.

### Option A: Use Your Current Location (Recommended)

This is the fastest way:

1. When the app asks for location permission, tap **"Allow"**
2. The app will automatically detect where you are
3. You'll see a list of nearby delivery locations (like buildings, offices, or landmarks)
4. Tap the one that matches where you want your food delivered

**What You'll See:**
- A map with your location marked
- A list of nearby places (for example: "Lagos Island Office Complex", "Victoria Island Mall")
- Each location shows the address and how close it is to you

#### What If No Locations Appear?

If the app says **"We Will Soon Be In Your City"**, it means we don't deliver to your area yet. Here's what you can do:

1. **Fill Out the Request Form**
   - Enter your full name
   - Enter your email address
   - Enter a nearby landmark (like "University of Ibadan" or "City Hospital")
   - Enter your city and country
   - Tap "Submit"

2. **What Happens Next?**
   - We'll save your details
   - When we expand to your area, you'll be the first to know!
   - You'll receive an email notification

3. **In the Meantime**
   - You won't be able to order yet
   - You can try a different location if you're traveling
   - Check back in a few weeks

---

### Option B: Search for an Address Manually

If you don't want to use GPS, or if you're ordering for a different location:

1. Tap "Search for Address"
2. Type the address in the search box (for example: "23 Adeola Odeku Street, Victoria Island")
3. Select the correct address from the suggestions
4. We'll check if we deliver to that area

**Can't Find Your Address?**
- Try searching for a nearby landmark or building name
- Make sure you have internet connection
- Try a broader search (just the neighborhood name)

---

### Changing Your Delivery Location

You can change where you want food delivered at any time:

1. From the home screen, tap your current location at the top
2. Select a different saved location, or tap "Add New Location"
3. Follow the same steps as above

**Common Reasons to Change Location:**
- You're at work instead of home
- You want food delivered to a friend's place
- You're traveling

## 4. Browsing Restaurants

Once you've set your location, you'll see a list of restaurants that deliver to you.

### Understanding the Restaurant List

Each restaurant card shows:

- **Restaurant Name** (for example: "Bukka Hut")
- **Branch Location** (for example: "Victoria Island")
- **Star Rating** (out of 5 stars)
- **Number of Reviews** (for example: "128 reviews")
- **Opening Hours** (for example: "Open until 10:00 PM")
- **Restaurant Logo or Photo**

### How to Find What You Want

#### Browse All Restaurants

- Scroll up and down to see all available restaurants
- Restaurants are sorted by relevance and popularity

#### Search for Something Specific

1. Tap the search icon at the top
2. Type what you're looking for:
   - Restaurant name (for example: "Bukka Hut")
   - Type of food (for example: "jollof rice", "chicken")
   - Cuisine type (for example: "Nigerian food")
3. See matching restaurants and menu items

#### Filter by Discounts

1. Look for the "Discounts" tab at the top
2. Tap it to see restaurants with special offers
3. Save money on your order!

---

### Understanding Restaurant Information

When you tap on a restaurant, you'll see:

#### Header Section
- Large photos of the restaurant
- Star rating and reviews
- Phone number (tap to call)
- Map showing the restaurant location

#### Opening Hours
- Days open (for example: "Mon-Sun")
- Opening time (for example: "8:00 AM")
- Closing time (for example: "10:00 PM")

**What if a restaurant is closed?**
- It will be grayed out in the list
- You can still view the menu, but you can't order
- Check back during opening hours

## 5. Ordering Food

Ready to order? Here's how to build your order step by step.

### Step 1: Browse the Menu

Inside a restaurant page:

1. **Category Tabs** - Scroll sideways to see different categories:
   - All (shows everything)
   - Rice Dishes
   - Soups & Swallows
   - Proteins
   - Drinks
   - And more...

2. **Menu Items** - Each item shows:
   - Food photo
   - Item name (for example: "Jollof Rice & Chicken")
   - Price (for example: "₦3,500")
   - Discount price if on sale (for example: "₦3,150")
   - Short description

### Step 2: Select Your Food

Tap on any menu item to see details and add it to your order.

#### Understanding the Food Detail Screen

- **Large Photos** - See what the food looks like
- **Full Description** - Ingredients and details
- **Price** - Current price (with discount if applicable)
- **Quantity** - How many you want (default is 1)

#### Adding Extras (Sides)

Many dishes let you add extra items:

| Extra Item | Typical Price |
|------------|---------------|
| Extra Plantain | ₦500 |
| Coleslaw | ₦300 |
| Moi Moi | ₦400 |

To add an extra:
1. Look for the "Extras" or "Sides" section
2. Tap the checkbox next to items you want
3. The price will update automatically

#### Changing Quantity

- Tap the **+** button to add more
- Tap the **-** button to reduce (minimum is 1)
- The total price updates automatically

**Example:**
```
Jollof Rice & Chicken: ₦3,150
+ Extra Plantain: ₦500
+ Coleslaw: ₦300
= ₦3,950 per serving

Quantity: 2
Total for this item: ₦7,900
```

#### Tap "Add to Cart"

When you're happy with your selection, tap the "Add to Cart" button. You'll see a confirmation message.

---

### Step 3: Review Your Cart

Tap the cart icon (usually at the bottom or top of the screen) to see your order.

#### What's in Your Cart?

- List of all items you've selected
- Quantity for each item
- Price for each item
- Running total

#### Making Changes

| Action | How to Do It |
|--------|--------------|
| Increase quantity | Tap **+** next to the item |
| Decrease quantity | Tap **-** next to the item |
| Remove item | Swipe left on the item, or tap the trash icon |
| Clear entire cart | Look for "Clear Cart" button |

#### Ordering from Multiple Restaurants?

You can! Each restaurant will have its own cart. You'll check out separately for each one.

---

### Step 4: Checkout

When you're ready to order, tap the **"Checkout"** or **"Proceed to Checkout"** button.

#### Review Your Order

You'll see a summary page with:

**1. Delivery Location**
- Where your food will be delivered
- Tap to change if needed

**2. Order Summary**
- List of all items
- Subtotal (cost of food)

**3. Fees**
- **Service Fee** - A small percentage fee for using the platform (5% of your order total)
- **Delivery Fee** - A fixed cost to deliver your food (not a percentage)

**4. Coupon Code (Optional)**
- Have a promo code? Tap "Have a coupon code?"
- Enter the code and tap "Apply"
- See the discount applied to your order

**5. Payment Method**
- Pay with a saved card (if you've ordered before)
- Pay with a new card
- Some orders might be free (promotions)

#### Understanding Your Total

```
Subtotal (food cost):      ₦7,900
Service Fee (5%):          ₦   395
Delivery Fee:              ₦   500
Discount (if any):         ₦-  500
                           ---------
Total to Pay:              ₦8,295
```

---

### Step 5: Place Your Order

1. Review everything one more time
2. Tap **"Place Order"**
3. If paying by card, you'll see a secure payment screen
4. Enter your card details and confirm payment

#### What Happens Next?

**1. We Send Your Order to the Restaurant**
- Status: "Waiting for restaurant..."
- The restaurant has a few minutes to accept your order

**2. Restaurant Accepts Your Order**
- Status: "Order Accepted"
- You can no longer cancel without contacting support
- Now you'll pay (if you haven't already)

**3. Payment Processing**
- If you haven't paid yet, you'll complete payment now
- Status: "Processing Payment..."
- Once done: "Payment Complete"

**4. Restaurant Prepares Your Food**
- Status: "Preparing Your Food"
- The restaurant is cooking your order
- This usually takes 20-45 minutes depending on the restaurant

**5. Food is Ready**
- Status: "Ready!"
- Your food is ready for pickup or delivery

**6. Delivery**
- A delivery person picks up your food
- Status: "On the Way"
- You'll see an estimated arrival time

**7. Food Arrives**
- Status: "Delivered"
- Enjoy your meal!
- Don't forget to rate your experience

## 6. Tracking Your Order

You can check on your order at any time.

### How to Track

1. Tap "Orders" at the bottom of the screen
2. Tap on your current order
3. See real-time updates

### Order Status Meanings

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| 🟡 **Waiting** | We sent your order, waiting for restaurant to accept |
| 🟢 **Processing** | Restaurant confirmed and is preparing your order |
| �‍🍳 **Preparing** | Your food is being cooked |
| � **Ready** | Food is ready and waiting for pickup |
| 🚗 **Enroute Pickup** | Robot is on the way to collect your order |
| 🤖 **Robot Arrived for Pickup** | Robot has arrived at the restaurant |
| 🚚 **Enroute Delivery** | Robot is bringing your order to you |
| 🏠 **Robot Arrived for Delivery** | Robot is at your location - please collect! |
| ✅ **Completed** | Your order has been successfully delivered |
| 🔄 **Refunded** | Order was refunded |
| ❌ **Rejected** | Restaurant couldn't fulfill the order |
| ⛔ **Cancelled** | Order was cancelled |

### Robot Delivery

Fainzy uses robots for delivery! Here's what to expect:

**When Your Order is Coming:**
- You'll see real-time robot tracking
- Get notified when the robot is nearby
- "Please be at alert to receive your order from our robot"

**When Robot Arrives:**
- You'll get a notification: "Robot arrived to deliver your order. Please go collect..."
- Meet the robot at your delivery location
- Collect your sealed food package

**Tips for Robot Delivery:**
- Keep your phone nearby for notifications
- Be ready to meet the robot when it arrives
- Check that your food seal is intact

### What If Something Goes Wrong?

#### Restaurant Rejects Your Order

Don't worry! This rarely happens, but if it does:
- You'll get a notification immediately
- Any money charged will be refunded
- You can:
  - Try ordering from the same restaurant again
  - Choose a different restaurant
  - Cancel and try later

#### Order is Taking Too Long

- Check the estimated arrival time in the app
- If it's past the estimated time, tap "Contact Support"
- You can also call the restaurant directly from the order details

#### Need to Cancel Your Order?

You can cancel if:
- Status is "Waiting for Restaurant" - Full refund, no problem
- Status is "Order Accepted" - Contact support to cancel
- Status is "Preparing" or later - Cannot cancel (food is being made)

**How to Cancel:**
1. Go to Order Details
2. Look for "Cancel Order" button
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Refund will be processed within 5-10 business days

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Since we use phone number login, just log out and log back in with your phone number. You'll receive a new code. If you need to update your account password, go to Account → Change Password.

**Q: Can I use the same account on multiple phones?**
A: Yes! Just log in with your phone number on any device. Your saved addresses and order history will sync.

**Q: I changed my phone number. How do I update it?**
A: Contact Fainzy support through the app or email. They'll help verify your identity and update your number.

---

### Location & Delivery

**Q: Why can't I find my exact address?**
A: We deliver to specific service areas (usually major buildings, offices, and landmarks). Try searching for a nearby building or landmark that's close to you.

**Q: Can I order for someone else?**
A: Yes! When checking out, make sure the delivery location is where they are. You can also add special instructions like "Deliver to John at reception."

**Q: I moved to a new city. Can I still use Fainzy?**
A: If we deliver to your new city, just update your location. If not, you can submit a location request and we'll notify you when we expand there.

**Q: How do I know if you deliver to my area?**
A: The app will automatically check when you enter your location. If we don't deliver there, you'll see the "We Will Soon Be In Your City" message.

---

### Ordering

**Q: Can I order from multiple restaurants at once?**
A: You can browse multiple restaurants, but you'll need to place separate orders and pay separate delivery fees for each restaurant.

**Q: What if an item I want is sold out?**
A: Sold out items will be marked "Sold Out" and you won't be able to add them to your cart. Check back later or choose something else!

**Q: Can I customize my order (like "no onions")?**
A: Some restaurants allow special instructions. Look for a "Special Instructions" box when adding items to your cart. Type your request there.

**Q: Is there a minimum order amount?**
A: It depends on the restaurant. If there's a minimum, you'll see it when checking out.

---

### Payment

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- Debit cards
- Some local payment methods (varies by country)

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment processing. We never store your full card details on our servers.

**Q: Why was my card charged before the restaurant accepted my order?**
A: For some orders, we authorize the payment immediately to ensure funds are available. If the restaurant rejects your order, you'll be fully refunded within 5-10 business days.

**Q: How do refunds work?**
A: Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-10 business days. The exact timing depends on your bank.

---

### Delivery

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A: Most orders arrive within 30-60 minutes. The estimated time is shown when you place your order and in your order tracking.

**Q: Who delivers my food?**
A: Our network of trusted delivery partners picks up your food from the restaurant and brings it to you.

**Q: What if my food arrives cold or wrong?**
A: Contact support immediately through the app. We'll work with the restaurant to make it right, which may include a partial or full refund.

**Q: Do I need to tip the delivery person?**
A: Tipping is optional but appreciated! You can add a tip during checkout or give cash when they arrive.

---

### Technical Issues

**Q: The app keeps crashing. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Close the app completely and reopen it
2. Check for app updates in your app store
3. Restart your phone
4. If still not working, contact support

**Q: I'm not receiving the verification code.**
A:
1. Make sure you have phone signal
2. Check that you entered the correct phone number
3. Wait 30 seconds and tap "Resend Code"
4. Check if your phone is blocking unknown senders
5. If still nothing, contact support

**Q: The map isn't loading.**
A:
1. Make sure you have internet connection
2. Check that location permissions are enabled in your phone settings
3. Try searching for your address manually instead

## Safety & Security

### Food Safety

- Restaurants are vetted before joining Fainzy
- Food is sealed by the restaurant before delivery
- If seals are broken, don't accept the delivery and contact support

### Personal Safety

- We verify all delivery partners
- Share your location only with the app
- Don't share personal information with delivery persons
- Report any suspicious behavior immediately

### Account Security

- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your login details
- Log out if using a shared device
- Enable biometric login (fingerprint/face) if available
